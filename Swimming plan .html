Day 1: Warm-up + Freestyle Swim

    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Swim freestyle for 20 minutes, focusing on maintaining a steady pace and proper breathing technique.


    Day 2: Backstroke and Core Workout
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Swim backstroke for 20 minutes, focusing on maintaining proper form and rhythm.
    Perform a core workout including exercises such as planks, Russian twists, and bicycle crunches.


    Day 3: Rest and Recovery
    
    Day 4: Warm-up + Breaststroke Swim
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Swim breaststroke for 20 minutes, focusing on using proper kick and arm movements.


    Day 5: Endurance Swim
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Swim a combination of freestyle, backstroke, and breaststroke for 30-40 minutes, focusing on maintaining a steady pace.


    Day 6: Strength and Conditioning
    
    Perform dryland exercises to improve overall strength and conditioning, such as push-ups, lunges, squats, and shoulder presses.
    Include stretching exercises for flexibility.


    Day 7: Rest and Recovery
    
    Day 8: Warm-up + Butterfly Swim
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Swim butterfly stroke for 15-20 minutes, focusing on proper arm and leg coordination.  


    Day 9: Interval Training
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Perform a series of swimming intervals, alternating between high-intensity sprints and recovery periods.
    Total swimming time: 20-30 minutes.

    Day 10: Rest and Recovery
    
    Day 11: Warm-up + Kickboard Workout
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Use a kickboard for a kick-focused workout, kicking across the pool for 20 minutes.
    Include different kicks such as flutter kick, dolphin kick, and breaststroke kick.


    Day 12: Endurance Swim
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Swim continuously for 40-50 minutes, incorporating different strokes and focusing on maintaining a steady pace.


    Day 13: Strength and Conditioning
    
    Perform dryland exercises focusing on upper body strength, such as pull-ups, push-ups, and tricep dips.
    Include stretching exercises for flexibility.


    Day 14: Rest and Recovery
    
    Day 15: Warm-up + Individual Medley (IM)
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Swim individual medley, combining all four competitive strokes (butterfly, backstroke, breaststroke, and freestyle) in a predetermined order.
    Swim for 20-30 minutes, focusing on maintaining proper technique for each stroke.


    Day 16: Endurance Swim
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Swim continuously for 45-60 minutes, focusing on maintaining a steady pace and incorporating different strokes.

    Day 17: Rest and Recovery
    
    Day 18: Warm-up + Open Water Swim
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    If possible, swim in an open water environment (lake, ocean, etc.) for a duration of 30-45 minutes.
    Ensure safety precautions are taken and swim within designated areas.

    Day 19: Speed and Agility
    
    Warm up with stretching exercises for 5-10 minutes.
    Incorporate speed and agility drills such as ladder drills, quick feet exercises, and short sprint intervals in the water.


    Day 20: Rest and Recovery
